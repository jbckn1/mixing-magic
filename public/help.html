Important Information and User Specification

Mixing Magic must be played on a 1920 x 1080 resolution monitor. Using a monitor not meeting this standard could have unintended results, such as the beaker appears in unintended places. In addition, only one player should join as the scientist and coach! We do not have safeguards at this moment protecting multiple people from joining, which will be coming in the next update. When someone joins as the scientist, the beaker values are changed!

Game Description

Mixing Magic is a point-and-click game that has two players working together to recreate the color, volume, temperature, and pH of a randomly generated beaker. In addition to the two players, multiple spectators may join the game and spectate the interactions of the two players with the opportunity to provide hints to the players.

Game Objective

The objective of the game will be for the scientist and the coach to work together to recreate the color, volume, temperature, and pH of the beaker on the coach's screen to the beaker on the scientist screen.

Communication

On the right-hand side of every role, there will be a chat box for communication between the players. Each participate will have the name they choose at the login screen appear and the time when they send a message

Scientist Role

The scientist will be tasked with operating the buttons to measure and change the volume, temperature, pH, and color of the beaker they see on their screen. The scientist will have buttons to measure the volume, measure the tempetaure, measure the ph, change the volume, change the tempertaure, change the ph, change the color, and to compare the two beakers.

Coach Role

The coach will be provided information about the scientist’s beaker including the volume, temperature, pH, and color of the beaker and information about the beaker they need to help the scientist recreate.

Spectator's Role

Spectators will be able to see two beakers on their screen corresponding to the beakers for the scientist and the coach. The beakers will show the volume level and the color of the liquid for each beaker.

Understanding and Measuring liquid Volume

Liquid volume is the amount of space occupied by a liquid at rest. The volume of liquid can be measured by using several different methods. Mixing magic measures volume in beakers in milliliters. The scientist's beaker and the coach's beaker will both be assigned a random value between 0 ml to 400 ml. The volume of the coach's beaker will be provided on their screen in the output box. The volume of the scientist's beaker can be measured by pressing the measure volume button on the scientist's screen, which will display the volume on the coach's output box

Understanding and Measuring Temperature

Temperature is a physical quantity that expresses hot and cold. Mixing Magic uses the Celsius scale to measure temperature. The temperature range for each beaker goes from 0 ⁰C to 99 ⁰C.

Understanding and Measuring pH

PH is a scale used to specify the acidity or basicity of a liquid solution. Acidic solutions are measured in the range from 0 – 6. Neutral solutions have a pH of 7. Basic solutions have a pH level of 8 – 14. In normal circumstances, if you add liquid to a solution, the pH will lower. However, Mixing Magic disregards that fact to simplify the game.

Color Range in Mixing Magic

The beakers in Mixing Magic may range from red, blue, yellow, green, purple, orange, and pink.
